### No. 00

Most of my work sits between product engineering and frontend craft: interaction details, design systems, internal tools, and the product logic that makes the interface honest.

I know my way around systems too. Linux, shells, databases, deployment, the weird glue code that keeps things moving. I just prefer when that depth shows up in the product instead of becoming the product.

I spend a lot of time in Neovim, care about open source, and use AI heavily as part of my development loop. Not as a replacement for taste or judgment. More like a fast second brain for reading, shaping, testing, and cutting through busywork.
